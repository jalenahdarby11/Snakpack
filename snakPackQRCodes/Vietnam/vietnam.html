General Information

Country Name: Socialist Republic of Vietnam

Capital: Hanoi

Official Language: Vietnamese

Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND)

Population: Approx. 100 million

Time Zone: UTC +7:00 (Indochina Time)

Geography

Location: Southeast Asia, bordered by China, Laos, Cambodia, and the South China Sea

Area: 331,212 square kilometers (127,881 square miles)

Climate: Tropical in the south; monsoonal in the north with a hot, rainy season and a cool, dry season

Government

Type: Socialist one-party state

President: Võ Văn Thưởng (as of 2025)

Prime Minister: Phạm Minh Chính

Legislature: National Assembly

Independence: September 2, 1945 (from France)

Economy

GDP: $430 billion USD (nominal, 2022)

Major Industries: Electronics, textiles, footwear, agriculture, tourism, and seafood processing

Currency Exchange Rate: 1 USD ≈ 24,000 VND (approximate, may vary)

Tourism & Attractions

Popular Destinations: Ha Long Bay, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Hoi An, Hue, Sapa, and Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park

Culture: Deeply influenced by Confucianism, Buddhism, and French colonialism; known for traditional festivals, cuisine, and water puppetry

Fun Facts

National Motto: “Independence – Freedom – Happiness”

Famous For: Street food, rice terraces, conical hats (nón lá), and a long history of resilience and independence

Life Expectancy: Around 74 years
