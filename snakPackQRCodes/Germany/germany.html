General Information

Country Name: Federal Republic of Germany

Capital: Berlin

Official Language: German

Currency: Euro (EUR)

Population: Approx. 83 million

Time Zone: UTC +1:00 (Central European Time), UTC +2:00 (Daylight Saving Time)

Geography

Location: Central Europe, bordered by nine countries including France, Poland, and Austria. North Sea and Baltic Sea to the north.

Area: 357,022 square kilometers (137,847 square miles)

Climate: Temperate seasonal climate with moderate to heavy rainfall throughout the year.

Government

Type: Federal Parliamentary Republic

Chancellor: Olaf Scholz (as of 2025)

Legislature: Bicameral – Bundestag (Federal Diet) and Bundesrat (Federal Council)

Unification: October 3, 1990 (Reunification of East and West Germany)

Economy

GDP: $4.2 trillion USD (nominal, 2022)

Major Industries: Automotive, machinery, chemicals, electronics, and renewable energy

Currency Exchange Rate: 1 USD ≈ 0.91 EUR (approximate, may vary)

Tourism & Attractions

Popular Destinations: Berlin, Neuschwanstein Castle, the Black Forest, Munich (Oktoberfest), Cologne Cathedral, and the Romantic Road

Culture: Rich in art, music, philosophy, and literature; home to composers like Beethoven and Bach, and philosophers like Kant and Nietzsche.

Fun Facts

National Motto: “Einigkeit und Recht und Freiheit” (Unity and Justice and Freedom)

Famous For: Being Europe’s largest economy, precision engineering, beer culture, and contributions to classical music and philosophy

Life Expectancy: Around 81 years
