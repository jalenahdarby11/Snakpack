General Information

Country Name: United Mexican States (Estados Unidos Mexicanos)

Capital: Mexico City (Ciudad de México)

Official Language: Spanish

Currency: Mexican Peso (MXN)

Population: Approx. 126 million

Time Zones: Ranges from UTC -8 to UTC -5 depending on the region

Geography

Location: Southern portion of North America, bordered by the United States to the north, and Belize and Guatemala to the southeast

Area: 1,964,375 square kilometers (758,449 square miles)

Climate: Varies from tropical to desert; coastal areas are generally hot and humid, while inland areas can be temperate or arid

Government

Type: Federal Presidential Constitutional Republic

President: Andrés Manuel López Obrador (as of 2025)

Legislature: Bicameral Congress of the Union (Senate and Chamber of Deputies)

Independence: September 16, 1810 (from Spain)

Economy

GDP: $1.47 trillion USD (nominal, 2022)

Major Industries: Automotive, oil and petroleum, electronics, agriculture, tourism, and manufacturing

Currency Exchange Rate: 1 USD ≈ 17 MXN (approximate, may vary)

Tourism & Attractions

Popular Destinations: Cancun, Mexico City, Chichen Itzá, Tulum, Oaxaca, Guadalajara, and Copper Canyon

Cultural Heritage: Known for its rich history including ancient civilizations (Aztec, Maya), vibrant festivals, music, and cuisine

Fun Facts

National Motto: “¡Patria, Libertad, Trabajo y Cultura!” (Homeland, Liberty, Work, and Culture)

Famous For: Mariachi music, tacos, Día de los Muertos, lucha libre, and diverse indigenous traditions

Life Expectancy: Around 75 years
