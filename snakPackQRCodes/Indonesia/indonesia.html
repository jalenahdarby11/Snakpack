General Information

Country Name: Republic of Indonesia

Capital: Jakarta

Official Language: Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia)

Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)

Population: Approx. 275 million

Time Zones: UTC +7 to UTC +9 (multiple time zones)

Geography

Location: Southeast Asia and Oceania, between the Indian and Pacific Oceans. Composed of over 17,000 islands.

Area: 1,904,569 square kilometers (735,358 square miles)

Climate: Tropical climate with distinct wet and dry seasons, high humidity, and warm temperatures year-round.

Government

Type: Presidential Republic

President: Joko Widodo (as of 2025)

Legislature: People's Consultative Assembly (MPR), consisting of the Regional Representative Council and the People's Representative Council

Independence: August 17, 1945 (from the Netherlands)

Economy

GDP: $1.3 trillion USD (nominal, 2022)

Major Industries: Petroleum and natural gas, textiles, mining, palm oil, agriculture, and tourism

Currency Exchange Rate: 1 USD ≈ 15,000 IDR (approximate, may vary)

Tourism & Attractions

Popular Destinations: Bali, Jakarta, Yogyakarta, Komodo Island, Borneo (Kalimantan), Raja Ampat, and Borobudur Temple

Culture: A blend of indigenous traditions, Hindu-Buddhist heritage, Islamic influence, and Dutch colonial history; famous for gamelan music, batik, and traditional dances.

Fun Facts

National Motto: “Bhinneka Tunggal Ika” (Unity in Diversity)

Famous For: World's largest archipelago, rich biodiversity, unique cultures, and being one of the largest coffee producers

Life Expectancy: Around 72 years
